# Style Guard

Use this reference whenever polishing, rewriting, line editing, or responding to requests like "make it better", "make it more literary", "make it smoother", or "optimize the prose".

## Style Preservation Rule

Do not convert the manuscript into generic polished AI prose. Preserve the author's target style, genre promise, viewpoint texture, pacing, and character voice.

## AI-Prose Warning Signs

| Warning sign | Why it hurts |
|---|---|
| Every sentence becomes smooth and balanced | Removes tension and voice texture |
| Abstract emotional summaries increase | Reduces embodiment and scene presence |
| Characters speak in complete explanatory paragraphs | Flattens social reality and subtext |
| Too many poetic metaphors | Breaks genre pace and viewpoint credibility |
| Conflict becomes gentle and clarified | Weakens pressure |
| Ambiguity is explained away | Destroys suspense, irony, or theme |
| Distinct voices converge | Damages character identity |

## Rewrite Constraints

1. Keep key images if they carry theme, clue, or voice.
2. Keep plain sentences where speed, shock, comedy, or directness matters.
3. Do not replace culturally or character-specific phrasing with neutral phrasing unless clarity requires it.
4. Do not increase adjectives by default.
5. Do not make every emotion explicit.
6. Do not remove pauses, evasions, unfinished speech, or awkwardness when they reveal relationship.
7. Preserve genre rhythm: web fiction, literary fiction, suspense, romance, fantasy, and comedy do not want the same prose.
