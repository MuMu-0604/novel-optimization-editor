# Author Authorization Template

Use this reference before direct rewriting, structural changes, or any work where the author wants to grant modification permission.

## Short Authorization

```text
I authorize L1-L2 direct edits.
L3 requires a proposed plan first.
L4-L5 require my explicit confirmation.
Do not add major characters, world rules, deaths, romances, identity reveals, or ending changes.
Preserve event order and reveal order unless I approve a Sequence Impact Report.
```
