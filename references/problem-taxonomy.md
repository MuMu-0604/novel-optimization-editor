# Problem Taxonomy

Use this reference in Diagnosis Mode, Continuity Mode, Motivation Mode, Pacing Mode, Foreshadowing Mode, and Final QA Mode.

## Required Issue Format

```text
Issue ID:
Category:
Severity: minor / moderate / major / structural
Location:
Evidence from manuscript:
Reader-facing symptom:
Underlying cause:
Permission level:
Minimal fix:
Stronger fix:
Risk:
Needs author confirmation:
Records to update:
```

## Plot and Structure Issues

| Category | Check | Symptom | Typical fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Causal break | Does each event have a visible cause? | Events feel arbitrary | Add cause, seed trigger earlier, or reorder explanation |
| Missing prerequisite | Did the event require knowledge, trust, power, clue, or emotional state not yet earned? | Character acts before they could plausibly do so | Add prerequisite scene/beat or delay event |
| Scene without turn | Does the scene change information, power, emotion, plan, relationship, or stakes? | Scene feels skippable | Add a turn, merge, summarize, or cut |
| Repeated scene function | Does another scene already do the same job? | Pacing drag | Merge strongest material and preserve unique canon |
| Premature reveal | Does the scene expose a secret before suspense or setup is ready? | Later reveal weakens | Move line, hide source, convert to clue |
| Unpaid setup | Was a clue, promise, object, rule, wound, or relationship thread introduced without payoff? | Reader feels cheated | Add payoff, reframe as texture, or remove setup |
| Unseeded payoff | Does a major payoff appear without prior setup? | Twist feels random | Seed clue earlier or reduce payoff scale |
| Stakes plateau | Are risks not escalating across scenes? | Middle feels flat | Increase cost, deadline, exposure, or irreversible choice |

## Character Issues

| Category | Check | Symptom | Typical fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weak motivation | Is the character's desire concrete in this moment? | Choice feels writer-forced | Add goal, pressure, fear, value, or tradeoff |
| Knowledge leak | Does the character know something only the reader/other character knows? | Continuity break | Remove knowledge, add discovery, or move reveal |
| Emotional jump | Is the emotional transition earned beat by beat? | Reaction feels sudden | Add bodily reaction, memory trigger, resistance, or pause |
| Voice drift | Does diction match identity, relationship, mood, and era? | Everyone sounds alike | Restore character-specific vocabulary, rhythm, and silence |
| Arc shortcut | Does growth happen without cost or test? | Transformation feels unearned | Add failed attempt, consequence, or irreversible decision |

## Prose and Scene-Level Issues

| Category | Check | Symptom | Typical fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Abstract emotion | Is emotion named instead of embodied? | Low immersion | Replace some labels with action, perception, or choice |
| Weak verb | Is the verb generic when action matters? | Flat image | Use precise motion only where useful |
| Over-explaining | Does narration state what subtext already shows? | Reader feels guided too hard | Cut explanation or move it into implication |
| Rhythm monotony | Are sentence lengths and beats too uniform? | Reading feels mechanical | Vary sentence length, paragraph breaks, and action beats |
| Detail without function | Does description fail to support mood, plot, character, or foreshadowing? | Slow scene | Cut, sharpen, or attach detail to viewpoint |

## Severity Guide

```text
minor: local language or clarity issue
moderate: scene-level pace, motivation, or emotional clarity issue
major: affects chapter logic, character continuity, or foreshadowing
structural: affects timeline, reveal order, core arc, ending, or canon
```

Structural issues normally require L4 or L5 handling.
